Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

All orders are dispatched within 24–48 hours of payment confirmation. Delivery typically takes 3–5 business days across India, depending on your location. You'll receive a tracking link on WhatsApp once your order ships.

Do you ship across India?

Yes — we ship pan-India to all states and union territories. Free shipping is available on orders above ₹1,499. For orders below that, a standard shipping fee applies at checkout.

How do I know which size to pick?

Check the size chart on our Shop page — it covers chest, shoulder, length, and sleeve measurements in cm. If you prefer a relaxed, oversized fit, we recommend going one size up from your usual. Still unsure? Drop us a message and we'll help you out.

Can I return or exchange my order?

We do not accept returns on used or washed items. However, if you received a wrong item, a damaged piece, or a manufacturing defect, we'll replace it — no questions asked. Reach out within 7 days of delivery with your order details and we'll process it quickly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept UPI payments (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, and all UPI-enabled apps). More payment options are being added soon. For any payment issues, contact us directly.
